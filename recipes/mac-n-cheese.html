Panera Mac and Cheese

Here is a recipe for a copycat version of Panera's mac and cheese:

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Lumaconi pasta
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups milk
  • 4 ounces white American cheese
  • 8 ounces extra-sharp white Vermont cheddar
  • 1/4 teaspoon Dijon mustard (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Melt the butter in a large saucepan over low heat.
  3. Once melted, whisk in the flour and cook for 1 minute, whisking constantly to prevent burning.
  4. Slowly pour in the milk, whisking as you go.
  5. Increase the heat to medium and continue whisking until the sauce thickens and bubbles, about 4-5 minutes.
  6. Remove the pan from heat.
  7. Add the sliced American cheese, shredded white cheddar, Dijon mustard (if using), salt, and hot sauce (if using).
  8. Stir until the cheese melts.
  9. Add the cooked pasta to the cheese sauce. Stir to coat the pasta evenly, then return the saucepan to medium heat.
  10. Cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly, until the mac and cheese is warmed through.
  11. Serve immediately.
